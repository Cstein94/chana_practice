Meatballs and Spaghetti

Chana's Cooking Blog

Gluten Free, Dairy Free, Crockpot Dish

When my grandmother made Thanksgiving, besides the smell of turkey wafting from the oven, you could always count on her savory meatballs. Not only is this recipe mouthwatering, it's easy to make by just sticking in the crockpot. You will have a tasty but relaxing meal.

    Ingredients:
  • 1 lb ground meat
  • 4 Tbsp ketchup
  • 3 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 C brown sugar
  • 1 can tomato sauce (15 oz)
  • salt and pepper to taste
    Directions:
  1. mix all the ingredients together in a bowl
  2. roll the mixture into balls
  3. drop the balls into the crockpot
  4. cook on high for 3 hrs. or low for 7 hrs.
